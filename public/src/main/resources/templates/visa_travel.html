Travel to the UK

You have 30 days to enter the UK after your visa starts
Enter From Until

If you've booked to travel outside these dates, you should try to rearrange. If you cannot change your plans, you must apply for a replacement 30-day visa.

Travelling with your visa

You must use the passport you told us about in your visa application the first time you travel to the UK.

UK border officers will see your visa details when they scan your passport.
